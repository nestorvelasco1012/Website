La Nueva Tortilleria

Inventory and Management App.

This inventory management app helps the company handle its inventory. With efficient inventory tracking and real-time data access, this app streamlines the entire inventory management process. Whether it's managing the inventory for their four daily routes or keeping track of stock levels, the app provides a comprehensive and user-friendly interface.

Delivery drivers can easily access the app on their iPhones, while the production team utilizes iPads to ensure seamless coordination. By implementing Cloudkit and iCloud accounts, all the data is securely stored, and user access is effectively managed. The app displays all the products offered by the company, along with current inventory levels, with a helpful "low inventory" label to prompt action.

The barcode scanner feature further enhances efficiency by enabling quick product selection. Each daily route is accounted for, with details on the required number of corn tortilla packets for the day, as well as credits from unsold packets from the previous day. The app also allows for automatic adjustment of quantity based on box size, and delivery drivers can easily decrease inventory or mark down credits.

Production employees have the option to increase inventory based on the units they make. Administrators have full control and can manually update quantities for each product. The app keeps a detailed history of all updates made, including the action, user, and time of update.

Moreover, it offers an export option for current inventory and history, as well as access to credits history.

Temperature logs for refrigerators and freezers can be recorded, and administrators can access logs for any given date.

Additionally, the app provides a quick view of the last seven days of credits and daily information.

Overall, the inventory management app by La Nueva Tortilleria empowers the company to efficiently track their inventory, make informed decisions, and ensure that their customers always receive fresh and delicious tortillas.
